<p><p>In the reef tank market, there are two primary types: open type and closed type. In contrast, the closed type market operates with controlled environments, where parameters like temperature, salinity, and light intensity are meticulously managed, typically using advanced technology and equipment. Each type caters to different preferences and expertise levels among reef enthusiasts.</p></p>
