<p><p>The plastic honeycomb market consists primarily of two types: polypropylene honeycomb and polycarbonate honeycomb. Polypropylene honeycomb is lightweight, moisture-resistant, and offers excellent thermal insulation, making it suitable for automotive, construction, and packaging applications. On the other hand, polycarbonate honeycomb is known for its high impact resistance, optical clarity, and durability, making it ideal for applications requiring transparency and strength, such as skylights, safety glazing, and protective barriers. Both types contribute significantly to various industries by enhancing structural integrity and reducing weight.</p></p>
