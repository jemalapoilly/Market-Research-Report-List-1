<p><p>The vegetable fat powder market consists of various types, including coconut extract, palm extract, sunflower extract, and vanaspati extract, among others. Coconut extract is valued for its unique flavor and health benefits, while palm extract is known for its versatility and cooking properties. Sunflower extract is popular for its light taste and high unsaturated fat content, making it suitable for health-conscious consumers. Vanaspati extract, a hydrogenated fat, is widely used in traditional cooking, especially in South Asian cuisines. Other extracts may include oils from various seeds and nuts, catering to diverse culinary applications.</p></p>
