<p><p>B2B electronic commerce encompasses three market types: </p><p>1. **Buyer-oriented E-commerce** focuses on platforms where businesses seek suppliers, facilitating competitive bidding and procurement processes. </p><p>2. **Supplier-oriented E-commerce** involves suppliers showcasing products and services, attracting multiple buyers to streamline sales and distribution. </p><p>3. **Intermediary-oriented E-commerce** relies on intermediaries or third-party platforms that connect buyers and sellers, often providing added services like logistics and payment processing to facilitate transactions and enhance market access.</p></p>
