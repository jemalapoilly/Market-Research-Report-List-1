<p><ul><li>Right Angle Molecular Sieve Trap</li><li>In-line Molecular Sieve Trap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular sieve trap market consists of two primary types: right angle molecular sieve traps and in-line molecular sieve traps. Right angle molecular sieve traps are designed to adsorb impurities in a system with a 90-degree bend, optimizing space and efficiency. In-line molecular sieve traps, on the other hand, are integrated directly into the flow path of a process, providing effective filtration of contaminants as they pass through. Both types enhance the performance and longevity of various industrial applications by ensuring high purity levels.</p></p>
