<p><strong>The Nucleic Acid Transfection Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biochemical Transfection</li><li>Physical Transfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nucleic acid transfection reagents market is primarily divided into biochemical and physical transfection methods. Biochemical transfection involves the use of materials like lipids, polymers, or proteins to facilitate the delivery of nucleic acids into cells. This method is typically less invasive and achieves effective incorporation with lower cytotoxicity. In contrast, physical transfection encompasses techniques such as electroporation, microinjection, and gene gun methods, which use physical forces to introduce nucleic acids directly into the target cells, often providing high efficiency but with potential cell damage.</p></p>
