In contrast, 3D VOG provides a more comprehensive analysis by capturing eye movements in three-dimensional space, offering enhanced diagnostic capabilities for complex conditions. Both types serve crucial roles in clinical settings, research, and rehabilitation, catering to varying diagnostic needs.</p></p>
