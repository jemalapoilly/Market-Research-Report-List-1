<p><p>The enteric disease testing market consists of various types, each targeting specific pathogens. C. difficile testing focuses on diagnosing infections often linked to antibiotic use. Campylobacteriosis testing identifies bacterial infections common from contaminated food. Cholera testing is essential in areas with inadequate water sanitation. E. coli testing detects harmful strains associated with foodborne illnesses. H. pylori testing is crucial for identifying stomach infections that can lead to ulcers. Salmonellosis and Shigellosis testing are vital for diagnosing infections caused by these respective bacteria, often transmitted through contaminated food and water.</p></p>
