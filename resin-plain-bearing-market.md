<p><strong>The Resin Plain Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Sliding Bearing</li><li>Thrust Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin plain bearing market encompasses two primary types: radial sliding bearings and thrust sliding bearings. Radial sliding bearings facilitate smooth rotational motion and are designed to support radial loads, making them ideal for applications like motors and pumps. In contrast, thrust sliding bearings handle axial loads, providing stability and support in machinery where axial force is prevalent, such as in conveyors and rotary systems. Both types benefit from resin materials, offering reduced friction and enhanced durability in demanding environments.</p></p>
