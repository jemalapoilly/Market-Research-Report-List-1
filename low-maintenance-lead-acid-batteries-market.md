<p><p>The low maintenance lead-acid batteries market primarily consists of two types: valve-regulated lead-acid (VRLA) batteries and rechargeable sealed lead-acid batteries. VRLA batteries incorporate a pressure-regulated valve that recombines gases generated during charging, minimizing electrolyte loss and maintenance needs. Rechargeable sealed lead-acid batteries are designed to be fully sealed, preventing leakage and requiring no water addition. Both types offer reliability and longevity, making them ideal for applications in standby power, telecommunications, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
