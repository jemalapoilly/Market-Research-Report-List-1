<p><p>The reflective box liner market encompasses products designed to enhance packaging performance through void fill and cushioning solutions. Cushioning products, on the other hand, provide protective padding around fragile items, absorbing shocks and impacts. Together, these types of reflective liners improve insulation, temperature control, and overall package integrity, making them essential for shipping sensitive goods across various industries.</p></p>
