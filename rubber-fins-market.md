<p><p>The rubber fins market is characterized by two main types: monofins and traditional fins. Monofins feature a single, broad blade that enhances propulsion and is popular among advanced swimmers and divers. They provide streamlined movement, making them ideal for disciplines like freediving and underwater sports. Traditional fins, on the other hand, consist of two separate blades that provide versatility and are often favored by recreational snorkelers and swimmers. Each type caters to different preferences and skill levels in aquatic activities.</p></p>
