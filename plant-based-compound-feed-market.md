<p><p>The plant-based compound feed market encompasses various forms designed for animal nutrition, primarily including mash, pellets, and crumble. Mash is a coarse mixture of ground ingredients, easy for animals to consume. Pellets are compressed feeds that enhance digestibility and reduce waste, providing a balanced nutrient profile. Crumble, a slightly processed form of pellets, offers an intermediate texture, making it suitable for young animals. Other forms may include extruded feeds and liquid feeds, catering to specific dietary needs and preferences.</p></p>
