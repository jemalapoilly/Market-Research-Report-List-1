<p><p>The diagnostic audiometer market comprises three primary types: stand-alone audiometers, hybrid audiometers, and PC-based audiometers. Stand-alone audiometers are portable devices that operate independently for basic audiometric testing. Hybrid audiometers combine features of stand-alone and PC-based models, offering flexibility and enhanced testing capabilities. PC-based audiometers connect to a computer, utilizing software for advanced testing, data analysis, and reporting. Each type caters to different user needs, from simple assessments to comprehensive audiological evaluations, enhancing hearing diagnosis and rehabilitation.</p></p>
