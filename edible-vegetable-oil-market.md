<p><p>The edible vegetable oil market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics and uses. Soybean oil is versatile and widely used for cooking and baking. Peanut oil offers a high smoke point, ideal for frying. Rapeseed oil, including canola, is low in saturated fat, making it popular for healthy cooking. Olive oil is prized for its flavor and health benefits. Cottonseed oil is less common but used in processed foods. Corn oil is known for its light taste, while coconut oil is recognized for its unique flavor and health attributes. Nut oils are often used for gourmet dishes, and the "other" category includes various regional oils like sesame and sunflower.</p></p>
