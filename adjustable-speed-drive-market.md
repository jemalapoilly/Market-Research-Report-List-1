<p><p>The adjustable speed drive (ASD) market is segmented into three main types: AC drives, DC drives, and servo drives. AC drives control the speed and torque of AC motors using variable frequency and voltage. DC drives are used for controlling the speed of DC motors, offering precise speed regulation and easy integration. Servo drives are advanced systems that provide high-performance control for servo motors, enabling precise position and speed adjustments. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
