<p><p>The Hall Voltage Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage output that is proportional to the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurements. In contrast, Digital Output Hall Sensors deliver discrete signals, indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field. This binary output is ideal for applications like position sensing and proximity detection, enhancing control and automation processes.</p></p>
