<p><p>The Single-Use Centrifuge Machines market is primarily categorized into two types: Benchtop Centrifuges and Continuous Flow Centrifuges. Benchtop Centrifuges are compact and designed for small-scale, laboratory use, making them ideal for research and clinical applications where ease of use and portability are essential. In contrast, Continuous Flow Centrifuges are engineered for high-capacity processing, allowing for the uninterrupted separation of biological materials. This is particularly beneficial in industrial or large-scale production settings, enhancing efficiency and throughput.</p></p>
