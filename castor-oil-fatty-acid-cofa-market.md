<p><p>Castor Oil Fatty Acid (COFA) is derived from castor oil and is categorized into two main types: Ordinary Castor Oil Fatty Acid and Dehydrated Castor Oil Fatty Acid. Ordinary COFA is extracted directly and retains a higher moisture content, leading to applications in soaps and lubricants. Dehydrated COFA, processed to eliminate water content, is favored in industrial applications due to its enhanced stability and lower viscosity, making it suitable for coatings, adhesives, and cosmetics. Both types serve diverse sectors and markets.</p></p>
