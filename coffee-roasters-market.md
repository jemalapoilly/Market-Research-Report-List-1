<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Coffee Roasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Fire</li><li>Semi-Direct Fire with Half Hot Air</li><li>Hot-Air</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coffee roasters are categorized into several market types based on their heating methods. Direct-fire roasters utilize flames to heat the beans directly, imparting a smoky flavor. Semi-direct fire roasters combine direct heat and half hot air, offering a balance of flavor and even roasting. Hot-air roasters use heated air to circulate around the beans, resulting in a cleaner taste with less oily residue. Other types may include hybrid systems or specialized equipment tailored for specific roasting profiles or artisan production.</p></p>
