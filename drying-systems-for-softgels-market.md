<p><strong>The Drying Systems for Softgels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumbler Dryer & Drying Tunnels</li><li>In-Line Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drying systems for softgels play a crucial role in pharmaceutical manufacturing, ensuring the removal of moisture to maintain product stability. Tumbler dryers utilize a rotating drum to evenly distribute heat, promoting uniform drying of softgels. Conversely, drying tunnels employ a continuous conveyor system, subjecting softgels to controlled airflow and temperature for efficient moisture removal. In-line drying integrates drying processes within the production line, enhancing efficiency and reducing handling, thereby optimizing the overall softgel manufacturing process.</p></p>
