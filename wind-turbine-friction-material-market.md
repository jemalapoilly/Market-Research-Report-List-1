<p><p>The wind turbine friction material market comprises various types of brake pads essential for turbine operation and safety. Organic brake pads are made from natural materials, offering quiet performance and reduced environmental impact but lower durability. Sintered brake pads, created from metal particles combined under heat and pressure, provide excellent strength and heat resistance, making them suitable for high-stress applications. Composite brake pads, combining various materials, balance performance and weight, delivering effective braking with longer life and versatility in diverse conditions.</p></p>
