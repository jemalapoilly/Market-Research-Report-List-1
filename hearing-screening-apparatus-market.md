<p><p>The Hearing Screening Apparatus market primarily includes three types: Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE), Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR), and other examinations. OAE devices assess cochlear function by measuring sound emissions from the inner ear, ideal for newborn and pediatric screening. ABR tests evaluate the auditory nerve and brainstem response to sound, suitable for more comprehensive hearing assessments. The 'Other' category encompasses various alternative methods and technologies for hearing evaluation, expanding the options for early detection and diagnosis of hearing impairments.</p></p>
