<p><p>The incremental rotary encoder market is segmented into three types: mechanical, optical, and magnetic encoders. Mechanical encoders use physical contacts to detect position changes, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with wear limitations. Optical encoders utilize light sensors and coded discs for high precision and durability, making them suitable for applications demanding accuracy. Magnetic encoders employ magnetic fields to detect rotational position, providing robustness in harsh environments. Each type caters to different industry needs based on performance, precision, and durability requirements.</p></p>
