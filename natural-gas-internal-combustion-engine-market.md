<p><ul><li>Spark Ignition</li><li>Compression Ignition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas internal combustion engine market comprises two main types: spark ignition and compression ignition engines. Spark ignition engines utilize a spark plug to ignite a mixture of air and natural gas, making them common in light-duty vehicles and smaller applications. In contrast, compression ignition engines rely on high compression to ignite the fuel-air mixture, typically used in heavy-duty vehicles and industrial applications. Both types offer benefits such as reduced emissions and improved fuel efficiency, enhancing their market appeal.</p></p>
