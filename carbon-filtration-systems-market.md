<p><p>Carbon filtration systems utilize carbon-based materials to remove contaminants from air and water. The market primarily includes granular activated carbon (GAC) and block carbon types. GAC offers high adsorption capacity and versatility, suitable for treating larger volumes, while block carbon is more compact, providing efficient filtration for smaller applications. Both types are effective in eliminating impurities, odors, and chemicals, making them vital in various settings, including residential, industrial, and municipal water treatment, as well as air purification systems.</p></p>
