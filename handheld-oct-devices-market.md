<p><strong>The Handheld OCT Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral Domain OCT</li><li>Scanning Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld OCT devices primarily fall into two market types: Spectral Domain OCT and Swept Source OCT. Spectral Domain OCT uses broad-spectrum light to capture high-resolution images, enabling detailed imaging of retinal structures and tissues. In contrast, Swept Source OCT employs a tunable laser source that rapidly scans wavelengths, offering deeper penetration and faster imaging, particularly beneficial for observing deeper ocular structures. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities while improving patient comfort due to their portable and non-invasive nature.</p></p>
