<p><p>Diamond slurries, essential for precision grinding and polishing in various industries, are categorized into four main types based on their carrier medium. Water-based slurries are environmentally friendly and offer easy cleanup. Lubricant-based slurries provide enhanced performance and reduced friction. Alcohol-based slurries excel in applications requiring rapid drying and are often used in electronics. Oil-based slurries deliver superior lubrication and are ideal for metalworking, ensuring a smooth finish. Each type is tailored for specific applications and user needs.</p></p>
