<p><strong>The Dental X-Ray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-rays</li><li>Extraoral X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray market comprises two primary types: intraoral and extraoral X-rays. Intraoral X-rays are taken within the mouth, providing detailed imagery of individual teeth and surrounding structures, crucial for diagnosis and treatment planning. Conversely, extraoral X-rays are captured outside the mouth, offering broader perspectives of the jaw, skull, and overall dental structure, useful for assessing orthodontic issues and comprehensive oral health. Both types play essential roles in modern dentistry, enhancing patient care and treatment accuracy.</p></p>
