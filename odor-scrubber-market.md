<p><p>The odor scrubber market is divided into two main types: wet and dry scrubbers. Wet scrubbers utilize liquid solutions to capture and neutralize odors, primarily through absorption or chemical reactions, making them effective for various industrial applications. In contrast, dry scrubbers employ adsorbent materials or chemical processes to eliminate odors without the use of liquids, offering a compact and efficient solution. Both types cater to distinct needs, with wet scrubbers often being used for high-humidity environments and dry scrubbers being favored for space efficiency.</p></p>
