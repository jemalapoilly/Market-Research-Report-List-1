<p><p>Fertilizer testing services encompass two main market types: physical analysis and chemical analysis. Physical analysis assesses the physical properties of fertilizers, such as granule size, density, and moisture content, which affect handling and application. Chemical analysis, on the other hand, evaluates the nutrient composition, including macro and micronutrients, ensuring that fertilizers meet regulatory standards and efficacy for agricultural use. Both analyses are essential for optimizing fertilizer performance, enhancing crop yield, and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
