AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance) sensors offer high sensitivity to magnetic fields, providing reliable detection. GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) sensors enhance performance with greater sensitivity and accuracy. TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance) sensors deliver superior detection capabilities with low power consumption. Other types may include optical or capacitive sensors. Each technology serves to enhance the safety and efficiency of railway switching operations.</p></p>
