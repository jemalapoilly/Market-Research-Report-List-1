<p><p>The handheld airborne particle counter market is divided into two main types: ionizing and non-ionizing counters. Ionizing counters utilize charged particles to detect and measure airborne contaminants, making them effective for detecting smaller particles. In contrast, non-ionizing counters operate using light scattering or optical methods, making them suitable for larger particles and providing real-time measurements. Both types serve various industries, including pharmaceuticals, cleanrooms, and environmental monitoring, ensuring air quality control and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
