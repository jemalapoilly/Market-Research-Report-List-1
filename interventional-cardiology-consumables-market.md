<p><p>The Interventional Cardiology Consumables Market encompasses various devices vital for cardiac procedures. Coronary stents are mesh tubes used to keep arteries open after angioplasty. PTCA balloon catheters are inflatable devices that dilate narrowed vessels during angioplasty. Guide wires serve as a pathway for these catheters, allowing precise navigation within the cardiovascular system. Other consumables may include accessories like sheaths, introducers, and filter devices, each contributing to the success and safety of interventional cardiac procedures.</p></p>
